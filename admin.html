How to Edit Content

Step 1: Go to any page on your website (Home, Collection, Product, etc.)

Step 2: Hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) and click on any text you want to edit

Step 3: A popup will appear - type your new text and click OK

Step 4: Your changes are saved as DRAFT - only you can see them

Step 5: When ready, click "Deploy All Changes" button (in admin bar or dashboard) to publish to all users

Note: Look for elements with a dashed outline - these are editable. Regular users never see your draft changes until you deploy.
