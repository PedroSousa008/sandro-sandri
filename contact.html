FAQ

Common Questions

We offer complimentary shipping on all orders within the EU. Orders typically ship within 2-3 business days and arrive within 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee.

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags attached. Please contact us to initiate a return. We offer free returns within the EU.

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up. Our customer service team is also happy to assist with sizing questions—just send us an email with your measurements.

Yes, we offer complimentary alterations on all blazers and coats purchased at full price. For other pieces, we can recommend trusted tailors in your area. Contact us for more details.

Each garment comes with specific care instructions on the label. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for tailored pieces and gentle hand washing for knitwear. Hang garments on proper hangers to maintain their shape.
