Our Story

Sandro Sandri

Dreamed in Italy, Produced in Portugal, and worn by a few

The Beginning

Born from a dream of effortless elegance

Sandro Sandri was born from a simple observation: true elegance lies not in following trends, but in wearing pieces that transcend time. Our founder, inspired by the sartorial traditions of Milan and the master craftsmen of Porto, set out to create a brand that would bridge these two worlds.

Each collection tells a story—a chapter in our ongoing exploration of what it means to dress with intention. We believe that the clothes we wear should be as considered as the life we lead.

The Founder
Craftsmanship

Where tradition meets innovation

Every Sandro Sandri piece is handcrafted in our partner ateliers in northern Portugal, where generations of artisans have perfected their craft. We work exclusively with master tailors who share our uncompromising standards for quality.

From the initial sketch to the final stitch, each garment passes through over 100 hands. This meticulous process ensures that every piece meets our exacting standards—garments worthy of becoming treasured possessions.

The Atelier
Materials

The finest fabrics from Europe's heritage mills

We source our fabrics from the finest mills in Italy, Portugal, and Belgium— suppliers who have been perfecting their craft for centuries. Our wool comes from Portuguese flocks, our linen from Belgian fields, and our cotton from Egyptian delta.

We never compromise on materials. Every fabric is selected not just for its beauty, but for its ability to age gracefully, becoming more beautiful with each wear.

The Fabrics
Sustainability

Slow fashion, by design

True sustainability isn't about trends or certifications—it's about creating pieces that last. Every Sandro Sandri garment is designed to be worn for years, even decades. We believe the most sustainable choice is to buy less, but buy better.

Our limited production runs ensure minimal waste, while our local supply chain keeps our carbon footprint small. We repair, we restore, and we stand behind every piece we create.
