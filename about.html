What is Sandro Sandri?

Dreamed in Italy, produced in Portugal and worn by a few

A statement of refined simplicity, Sandro Sandri embraces a vision where elegance is effortless, and exclusivity is essential. Each piece is crafted in limited quantities, designed for those who seek sophistication beyond trends, where fashion is not mass-produced, but thoughtfully created.

Welcome to our illustration and refined society, where exclusivity meets excellence in every aspect. Within this exclusive enclave, you'll discover a sanctuary where Luxury, Culture, Art and Comfort blend harmoniously.

Collections

Each of the four tell a unique story

Voglia di Viaggiare - for those who see travel as a philosophy, a journey of discovery and beauty.

Connoisseur - a piece for those who appreciate life's finest details, where taste and elegance intertwine.

L'Italia per un viaggio indimenticabile - the spirit of Italian charm, evoking the allure of an unforgettable escape.

Rinascimento Couture - a tribute for artistic rebirth, blending heritage with modern refinement.

Founder

The vision behind Sandro Sandri

At 22, Pedro shaped the brand around a quiet, timeless aesthetic - where every detail carries meaning and every piece belongs to a larger story.

Guided by a deep appreciation for simplicity, craftsmanship, and the emotional power of beautiful places, he transformed his inspirations into collectible Chapters that define the identity of Sandro Sandri. His approach is subtle yet exacting, insisting that true luxury lives in restraint, quality, and purpose.

The Craft

Objects of Identity, Crafted to Endure

More than simple packaging, the Sandro Sandri Boxes are designed as timeless objects—pieces of decoration that carry the spirit, history, and quiet elegance of the brand. Each box is crafted with intention: the precise tones, the refined textures, the studied balance of typography and space. They are created to be kept, displayed, and lived with.

Every chapter of the brand introduces a new Box, turning these pieces into a continuing archive of the Sandro Sandri universe. They are collectible markers of each moment, designed not to hold an item, but to preserve a story. In their simplicity and permanence, the Boxes embody what defines the brand itself: restraint, beauty, and a lasting sense of purpose.
